Frequently Asked Questions

What are the prerequisites for joining?

No prior coding experience required for beginner courses. Basic computer knowledge and passion to learn are enough. For advanced courses, basic programming knowledge is recommended.

What is the duration of the course?

Course duration varies from 3 to 6 months depending on the program. Full Stack Development is 6 months, while specialized courses range from 3-4 months.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we provide 100% placement assistance including resume building, mock interviews, and direct placement opportunities with our 100+ partner companies.

What is the fee structure?

Course fees range from ₹29,999 to ₹49,999 depending on the program. We also offer scholarships and discounts for early birds and referrals.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible EMI options. You can pay in 3, 6, or 12 monthly installments with zero interest on selected payment methods.
